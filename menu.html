Our Menu

Appetisers:

  • Bruschetta - Our signature appetiser. Vine-ripened tomatoes on toasted bread with garlic, basil, olive oil.
  • Caprese Salad - tomato, buffalo mozarella, basil.
  • Antipasto Platter - Perfect for sharing. Cured meats, creamy and robust cheeses, olives, marinated vegetables.

Main Courses:

  • Spagetti Carbonara - made with fine Kerry bacon rashers
  • Lasagna - a Neopolitan classic made with the finest irish beef.
  • Gnocchi with spinach and roasted red peppers.
  • Penne alla Vodka - A signature dish with the blending of cultures. Tomato, cream, basil, parmesan, with a perfume of vodka.

Desserts

  • Tiramisu: A coffee-flavored dessert made of ladyfingers dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar, and mascarpone cheese. Flavored with cocoa and dark rum for a twist on the classic.
  • Pistachio Cannoli: sweet and nutty.
  • Panna Cotta: A creamy, set dessert made with sweetened cream and gelatin, often served with fruit coulis or caramel sauce.
  • Gelato: Our flavours include pistachio, hazelnut, stracciatella, chocolate and vanilla.
  • Amaretto: Almond cookies, crisp on the outside and slightly chewy inside.
